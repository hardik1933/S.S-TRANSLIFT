Design a modern logistics and transport management SaaS dashboard for a company called:

S.S. Translift
Location: Kalamboli, Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

The system should digitize the company’s current workflow where they manage transport shipments using Excel sheets and manual registers. The company works with parties and brokers to move containers using trucks and sometimes hires external trucks and gives them a freight cut.

Design Style:
Modern SaaS dashboard, minimal clean UI, logistics theme, card-based layout, responsive for desktop and mobile.
Color palette: Deep Blue (#0F172A), Industrial Orange (#F97316), Light Gray (#F8FAFC)
Typography: Inter / Poppins.

The design should feel like a professional logistics management system replacing manual registers.
